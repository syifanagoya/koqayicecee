DrDisRespect Wiki: Early Life, Parents, and Education

There isn’t much information about Dr. DisRespect’s childhood, such as names of his parents and whether he has any siblings or not, however, it is known that he received a degree from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, during which time he also played basketball for the university team, competing in Division II NCAA league.

Career Beginnings

As early as January 2010, Dr. DisRespect became known to the world, but first by his real name – the alias Dr. DisRespect was added later. He started by playing Halo 2 on the Xbox competing with other players, using voice chat and speaking aggressively to other players, and thanks to his great playing skills, he became a worthy opponent. With his roommates, Beahm started a YouTube channel “Dr DisRespect” on the 5th January 2010, with his first video “Dr DisRespect Gaming: The Docs Very 1st Video”, posted on the 11th January. Since then he has gained over 700,000 subscribers, and his videos have attracted more than 29 million views. As he continued to upload videos, DrDisRespect became more popular, his videos featuring in the popular web show “Machinima”. However, he left YouTube in 2011 and didn’t post anything more until 2016.

Also in 2011, he became a community manager of Sledgehammer Games, and ultimately reached the position of designer, helping design several maps for the popular game “Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare”. However, once he became popular on Twitch, he left Sledgehammer.

Twitch Career

DrDisRespect became popular even before Twitch was founded; he opened his channel on Justin.tv, which later became Twitch, and started uploading videos of himself playing various battle royale games, particularly focusing on H1Z1, and later switching to PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds. He has now gained more than 2.5 million followers, becoming extremely popular. He took a hiatus back in late 2017, but returned to streaming in February 2018, and with his first video attracted 388,000 concurrent viewers, which at the time was a record.

Modern Warfare 2 Multiplayer Maps

Just like the rest of the leaks, intel on the game's multiplayer maps was unveiled by leaker @RalphsValve, who revealed that several maps from the first Modern Warfare 2 would be making an appearance. Here's a rumoured list of remastered maps expected to appear:

  • Favela
  • Terminal
  • Highrise
  • Shipment
  • Quarry

As always, take leaks with a pinch of salt but eagle-eyed fans have spotted Highrise within the Modern Warfare 2 reveal trailer.

Following the reveal, Infinity Ward shared the first details of Modern Warfare 2 multiplayer. The game will contain Battle Maps and Core Maps. Battle Maps are for large-scale game modes while Core Maps are designed for the classic dose of 6v6 action players expect.

Here's a list of confirmed Core Maps appearing in Modern Warfare 2:

  • Museum
  • Grand Prix
  • Farm 18 Training Facility

Modern Warfare 2 Battle Maps

Battle Maps are likely to appear in Ground War and are areas from the new map appearing in Warzone 2. Here are all of the Modern Warfare 2 Battle Maps confirmed so far:

  • Sariff Bay - Set in fishing village.
  • Sa'id - Designed for "urban combat."

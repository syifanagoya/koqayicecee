Love &amp; Hip Hop spoilers: Infinity is still with Yandy after getting some rules

June 2024 · 2 minute read

On Season 9 of Love & Hip Hop, Yandy Smith took on another child and we’ve been watching that play out on camera. At first, it looked like Yandy was helping out during a tough situation but recently, it was looking more like she got taken advantage of by the teenage Infinity.

After talking to Infinity’s birth mother, Yandy had a lot of questions and even more concerns. Upon learning that Infinity’s story may not be completely true, she confronted the teenager and it was explosive.

There’s good news, though, for those who are following the story of Infinity and whether she will continue living a stable life in Yandy’s home. At least for now, it looks like she’ll be sticking around.

With a teenager in her house, it was time for Yandy to lay down some ground rules and most certainly, one of those rules would be a curfew.

After watching the drama play out on the last episode of Love & Hip Hop, there were some questions from viewers about what might happen to Infinity. Would she stay with Yandy or was her whole story a sham?

It turns out that Infinity is still around and living with Yandy. In fact, the Love & Hip Hop star recently shared a new photo on Instagram and it looks like Infinity is fitting in to her new life perfectly.

The photo features Yandy with both of her girls in celebration of Skylar’s birthday. In it, she wrote, “Who knew I’d be blessed with two girls.”

From the way things sound on social media, it looks like the drama we’re seeing play out on Love & Hip Hop has been dealt with as everyone looks and sounds very happy.

Even on Infinity’s Instagram, where she’s very active, there are quite a few pictures of her with Skylar, even referring to the little girl as her sister.

Based on what we’re seeing on social media, it looks like we’ll be seeing a lot more of Infinity because she’s definitely become a part of Yandy’s family.

Love & Hip Hop airs on Mondays at 8/7c on VH1.
